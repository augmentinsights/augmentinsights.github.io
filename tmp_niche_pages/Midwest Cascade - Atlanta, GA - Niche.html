editorial

Midwest Cascade is a neighborhood in Atlanta, Georgia with a population of 14,860. Midwest Cascade is in Fulton County. Living in Midwest Cascade offers residents an urban suburban mix feel and most residents own their homes. In Midwest Cascade there are a lot of parks. Many young professionals live in Midwest Cascade and residents tend to be liberal.
