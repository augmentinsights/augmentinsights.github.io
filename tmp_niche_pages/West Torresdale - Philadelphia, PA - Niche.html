editorial

West Torresdale is a neighborhood in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania with a population of 2,711. West Torresdale is in Philadelphia County. Living in West Torresdale offers residents an urban suburban mix feel and most residents own their homes. In West Torresdale there are a lot of coffee shops and parks. Many retirees live in West Torresdale and residents tend to be liberal.
