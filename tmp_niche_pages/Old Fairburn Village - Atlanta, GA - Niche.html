editorial

Old Fairburn Village is a neighborhood in Atlanta, Georgia with a population of 5,395. Old Fairburn Village is in Fulton County. Living in Old Fairburn Village offers residents a dense urban feel and most residents rent their homes. Many young professionals live in Old Fairburn Village and residents tend to be liberal.
