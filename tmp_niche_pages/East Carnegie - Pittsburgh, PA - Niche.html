editorial

East Carnegie is a neighborhood in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania with a population of 2,661. East Carnegie is in Allegheny County and is one of the best places to live in Pennsylvania. Living in East Carnegie offers residents a dense suburban feel and most residents own their homes. In East Carnegie there are a lot of restaurants and parks. Many young professionals and retirees live in East Carnegie and residents tend to lean liberal.
