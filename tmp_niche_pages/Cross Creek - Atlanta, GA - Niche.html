editorial

Cross Creek is a neighborhood in Atlanta, Georgia with a population of 3,282. Cross Creek is in Fulton County and is one of the best places to live in Georgia. Living in Cross Creek offers residents an urban feel and most residents own their homes. In Cross Creek there are a lot of restaurants and parks. Many young professionals live in Cross Creek and residents tend to be liberal. The public schools in Cross Creek are above average.
