editorial

Franklin Park is a neighborhood in Austin, Texas with a population of 20,906. Franklin Park is in Travis County. Living in Franklin Park offers residents a dense suburban feel and most residents rent their homes. In Franklin Park there are a lot of coffee shops and parks. Many families live in Franklin Park and residents tend to be liberal. The public schools in Franklin Park are above average.

    Rating 3 out of 5  
    We know the neighbors around us and some that live on the next few streets and we interact with them. However there is not much of a sense of community with everyone in the area it's kind of every man for themselves and the neighborhood doesn't work together to better the community. There is a small select group of moms who are newer to the neighborhood and are starting many initiatives to improve the quality of life of the neighborhood. They have remodeled the park, added lights to the school, increased the amount of street lights, are working with the few police officers in the neighborhood whom are interested in lowering the crime rate of children and adolescents. They also attempt to increase the amount of voters in the area and promote environmentally friendly initiatives of planting more trees. They haven't had much success with this neighborhood because of the amount of resistance to be involved in anything from the neighbors.
