editorial

Hallmark is a neighborhood in Louisville, Kentucky with a population of 2,934. Hallmark is in Jefferson County. Living in Hallmark offers residents an urban suburban mix feel and most residents own their homes. In Hallmark there are a lot of parks. Many families live in Hallmark and residents tend to be liberal.
