editorial

Violetville is a neighborhood in Baltimore, Maryland with a population of 6,203. Violetville is in Baltimore City County. Living in Violetville offers residents an urban suburban mix feel and most residents own their homes. In Violetville there are a lot of parks. Many families live in Violetville and residents tend to be liberal.
