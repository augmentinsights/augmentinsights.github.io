editorial

East Harriet is a neighborhood in Minneapolis, Minnesota with a population of 3,600. East Harriet is in Hennepin County and is one of the best places to live in Minnesota. Living in East Harriet offers residents an urban suburban mix feel and most residents own their homes. In East Harriet there are a lot of bars, restaurants, coffee shops, and parks. Many young professionals and retirees live in East Harriet and residents tend to lean conservative. The public schools in East Harriet are above average.
