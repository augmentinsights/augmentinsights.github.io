editorial

St. Anthony Park is a neighborhood in St. Paul, Minnesota with a population of 8,665. St. Anthony Park is in Ramsey County and is one of the best places to live in Minnesota. Living in St. Anthony Park offers residents an urban feel and most residents rent their homes. In St. Anthony Park there are a lot of bars, restaurants, coffee shops, and parks. Many young professionals live in St. Anthony Park and residents tend to have moderate political views. The public schools in St. Anthony Park are above average.
