editorial

Campbell Green is a neighborhood in Dallas, Texas with a population of 4,542. Campbell Green is in Dallas County and is one of the best places to live in Texas. Living in Campbell Green offers residents an urban suburban mix feel and most residents own their homes. In Campbell Green there are a lot of restaurants, coffee shops, and parks. Many young professionals live in Campbell Green and residents tend to lean conservative. The public schools in Campbell Green are highly rated.
