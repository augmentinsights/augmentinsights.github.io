editorial

Hartwell is a neighborhood in Cincinnati, Ohio with a population of 11,537. Hartwell is in Hamilton County. Living in Hartwell offers residents an urban suburban mix feel and most residents rent their homes. In Hartwell there are a lot of parks. Many families, young professionals, and retirees live in Hartwell and residents tend to be liberal. The public schools in Hartwell are above average.

      Rating 4 out of 5  
      I have lived here over 30 years. Houses are a bargain compared to other neighborhoods. Crime is very low, except that that car to car drug exchanges occur because we are so far from the Bond Hill District 4 police station. However, police from other municipalities will respond if something really serious occurs. Residents tend not to be active in the community council. There are many Victorian and Craftsman houses available. The business district is sound with a Kroger & Country Fresh on Vine St. There are numerous fast food restaurants. There is a new arts school which will be the home of the Contemporary Dance Theater in 2021. There is another new upscale business coming which is garden based. Residents are friendly. Hartwell seems to be on the upswing right now - houses are bought right away & many are being improved. It has been a pretty good place to live.
